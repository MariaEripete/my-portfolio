Books! Books! Books!

The picture represents the fact that i like to read. I read many types of books but my favourite ones are romace novels, selfhelp books, crime and mystery novels. These are such different genres but i still enjoy them equally.

I like to read books because it allow you to experience multiple realities. It is a great way to unwind when you have free time. It also dares you to grow because you learn a lot when reading, be it new vocabulary or new ideas, at the end of each reading session you come out more knowledgable than you were at the beginning of the session.

Reading also helps us remember. Reading isn’t only about discovering the new. It’s also a vehicle for reflection. Many bibliophiles can trace their love of reading back to a cherished memory, such as being read to by a parent or discovering the first book they ever loved (Winnie the Pooh, Dr. Seuss or Enid Blyton). Re-reading those favorite books, or reading about familiar places, times and people helps us recall the details of our own lives. It reminds us who we are, where we are and how we came here.
